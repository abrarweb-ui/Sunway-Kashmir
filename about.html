Who We Are

Your Kashmir Family

At Sunway Kashmir Tour & Travels, we believe Kashmir is not just a destination — it's a feeling. We are a team of passionate, born-and-raised Kashmiri travel professionals based in Srinagar, J&K.

We have helped over 500 families, couples, and groups experience the real Kashmir — from the lotus-dotted Dal Lake to the powdery slopes of Gulmarg, the meadows of Pahalgam to the glaciers of Sonmarg.
